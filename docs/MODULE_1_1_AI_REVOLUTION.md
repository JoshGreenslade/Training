### "Won't AI replace me?"

**No!** AI makes you more valuable, not less. Here's why:

- AI needs guidance (your prompts)
- AI needs verification (your review)
- AI can't make strategic decisions (your expertise)
- AI can't lead teams (your leadership)
- AI can't communicate with stakeholders (your translation)

You're not being replacedâ€”you're being **amplified**.

### "I prefer writing code myself"

That's fine for hobby projects! But professionally:

- Your time is valuable (and expensive)
- Routine code doesn't use your senior expertise
- AI can write boilerplate faster and more consistently
- You're more valuable in strategic roles

Would you rather spend your day:
- Writing CRUD endpoints? (AI can do this)
- Designing elegant architectures? (Only you can do this)

### "AI makes mistakes"

True! But so do humans. The key is:

- AI makes different mistakes than humans
- AI makes mistakes consistently (learnable patterns)
- AI can be retried instantly
- Your job is to catch those mistakes (quality gate)

Think of AI as a junior developer who:
- Types infinitely fast
- Never gets tired
- Has read all documentation
- Needs your guidance and review

### "This is too much to learn"

Actually, it's easier than you think:

- You already know software engineering
- AI-first is just a tool shift, not a paradigm shift
- This training teaches you step-by-step
- Start small, build confidence
- Everyone is learning together
